Institutional Overview

Established with the mandate of fostering intellectual rigor and ethical leadership, TBF Academy has remained at the forefront of secondary education for over a decade. Our institution is built upon the pillars of discipline, academic integrity, and the pursuit of holistic excellence.

We believe that education is not merely the accumulation of facts, but the training of the mind to think critically. Our faculty consists of seasoned educators dedicated to providing a competitive yet supportive environment that prepares students for the challenges of higher academia and global citizenship.

Admission Criteria & Standards

Admission to TBF Academy is strictly merit-based. We invite applications from students currently in or entering Classes 6 through 9. Candidates are evaluated through a multi-stage process involving a comprehensive entrance examination, an assessment of previous academic records, and a formal interview.

The entrance examination covers core competencies in Mathematics, Natural Sciences, English Language, and Analytical Reasoning. We seek students who demonstrate a high degree of curiosity and a commitment to our institutional values.
